# Engineering Blog Reading Map

## When to Use

Use quando um card de backend principle precisa virar caso real. Blogs de engenharia entram como fonte de exemplos, incidentes, migracoes e trade-offs, nao como lista infinita de leitura.

## What Breaks First

Importar blog demais cria biblioteca morta. O criterio de entrada e: o post ensina uma decisao, uma falha, uma migracao ou uma operacao que voce conseguiria defender em entrevista.

## Interview Trap

Citar empresa grande como autoridade. O ponto e extrair a decisao menor: requisito, restricao, trade-off, primeiro sinal e rollback.

## Practice Drill

Escolha um blog abaixo, selecione um post e preencha: problema, constraints, decisao, alternativa rejeitada, metrica de sucesso, como traduzir para empresa menor.

## Source Anchor

- [Netflix Tech Blog](https://netflixtechblog.com/): CDN, video delivery, resiliencia, data platform e operacao global.
- [LinkedIn Engineering Blog](https://www.linkedin.com/blog/engineering): data streams, search, feed, infra, scalability e distributed systems.
- [Meta Engineering Blog](https://engineering.fb.com/): core infra, data infra, production engineering, security e privacy.
- [Pinterest Engineering Blog](https://medium.com/pinterest-engineering): feed, recommendations, storage, search e data engineering.
- [Uber Engineering Blog](https://www.uber.com/in/en/blog/engineering/): marketplace, dispatch, payments, geospatial, workflows e platform engineering.
- [Slack Engineering Blog](https://slack.engineering/): migrations, reliability, developer experience e realtime collaboration.
- [AWS Architecture Blog](https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/architecture/): reference architectures, resilience, serverless, migration e well-architected trade-offs.
- [Microsoft Dev Blogs](https://devblogs.microsoft.com/): platform engineering, .NET, Azure, developer tools e cloud patterns.
- [Airbnb Tech Blog](https://medium.com/airbnb-engineering): infra, data platform, experimentation, search e product engineering.
- [GitHub Engineering Blog](https://github.blog/engineering/): Rails, MySQL, developer platform, availability, security e migrations.
- [Discord Blog](https://discord.com/blog): realtime, Elixir, infra, safety, media e client/server operations.
- [Spotify Engineering Blog](https://engineering.atspotify.com/): personalization, experimentation, platform, reliability e engineering culture.
- [Figma Engineering Blog](https://www.figma.com/blog/engineering/): multiplayer collaboration, files, performance, editor infra e product engineering.
